Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

glp3md ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect the following information:

  • First name
  • Email address

We do not collect sensitive medical information, protected health information (PHI), payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Add you to the retatrutide interest waitlist
  • Notify you when retatrutide receives FDA approval or becomes available for clinical use
  • Send relevant updates about the drug's regulatory and clinical status

We will not use your information for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Loops (loops.so) to manage our waitlist contacts and send email communications. Your first name and email address are transmitted to Loops for this purpose only. We do not authorize Loops to use your information for any other purpose.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any other third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. HIPAA Compliance

glp3md voluntarily follows privacy best practices consistent with HIPAA principles. The information collected on this Site — first name and email address — does not constitute protected health information (PHI). No PHI is collected, stored, or transmitted through this Site. We do not create, maintain, or transmit any medical records or health-related data.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission. Your name and email are stored securely by Loops in compliance with their security and privacy practices. Our waitlist API endpoint is protected server-side and your data is never exposed in client-side code.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you remain on the waitlist or until you request its removal. Once retatrutide becomes available and waitlist notifications have been sent, inactive contacts will be reviewed and removed on a reasonable schedule.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information at any time
  • Opt out of all communications using the unsubscribe link included in every email we send

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@glp3md.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking

This Site does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. Basic browser session storage may be used for standard page functionality. We do not build user profiles or track your behavior across other websites.

9. Children's Privacy

This Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated to waitlist members via email. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): the right to know what personal information is collected about you, the right to request deletion of your personal information, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. We do not sell personal information under any circumstances. To exercise any of your California privacy rights, contact us at hello@glp3md.com. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or data deletion requests, please contact us at hello@glp3md.com.
